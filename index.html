Toronto Team Game

Race to Union

Five teams. Five starting points. One HQ. Everyone walks to Union Station while HQ manually sends challenges and scores the chaos.

Format 5-team walking challenge
Finish Union Station, Front Street entrance
Target time 30-60 minutes

Fixed Setup

Team Routes

Starting locations are fixed. Teams choose their exact streets but must walk to Union Station.

HQ Controls

Challenge Pack

HQ edits and sends these manually during the race. Changes save in the browser automatically.

Game Rules

  • Teams must walk only.
  • Teams must stay together.
  • Proof must be photo or video.
  • Submit proof before arrival or immediately at Union.
  • Tiebreaker: creativity, then final proof time.
