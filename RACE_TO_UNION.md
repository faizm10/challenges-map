# Race to Union

## Summary

Race to Union is a 5-team walking challenge in Toronto. Each team starts from a different pre-defined location, completes 5 HQ-issued challenges during the walk, and finishes at Union Station. HQ scores teams based on arrival order, challenge completion, and creativity.

## Roles

- `HQ`: Sends challenges manually, collects proof, records arrival times, and assigns creativity scores.
- `Teams`: Stay together, walk to Union Station, complete challenges, and submit photo/video proof.

## HQ Challenge Pack

HQ enters and sends these manually during the game.

1. **Movie Trailer Shot**: Film a 10-second dramatic trailer for your team's race to Union.
2. **Toronto Meme**: Recreate a meme using something you find on the street.
3. **Stranger Cameo**: Get a stranger to say your team name on video.
4. **Landmark Proof**: Take a creative photo with a recognizable Toronto landmark.
5. **Chaotic Commercial**: Film a fake ad for a random everyday object.

## Scoring

Total score: **100 points**

### Speed: 40 points

- 1st to Union: 40
- 2nd: 32
- 3rd: 24
- 4th: 16
- 5th: 8

### Challenge Completion: 40 points

- 8 points per completed challenge with valid proof
- Maximum 40 points

### Creativity / Entertainment: 20 points

- HQ assigns 0-20 points based on originality, commitment, and entertainment value

### Tiebreaker

1. Higher creativity score
2. Earlier final proof submission time

## Game Flow

1. HQ assigns one team to each starting location.
2. HQ announces the finish point at Union Station before the start.
3. Teams begin walking at the same time.
4. HQ manually sends all 5 challenges during the race in the group chat.
5. Teams submit photo/video proof before arrival or immediately upon reaching Union Station.
6. HQ records arrival order and calculates final scores.

## Rules

- Teams must walk only.
- Teams must stay together.
- Teams may choose their exact streets, but must finish at Union Station.
- Proof must clearly show the team completed the challenge.
- Standard city safety rules apply at all times.

## Recommended Finish Point

Use one clearly defined meeting point, such as the **Front Street main entrance of Union Station**.

## Quick Start for HQ

- Create one group chat with all teams.
- Copy the 5 challenge prompts into a note for fast sending.
- Start a timer when the race begins.
- Record each team's arrival time and submissions in the score sheet.
- Announce final rankings after all teams arrive.
